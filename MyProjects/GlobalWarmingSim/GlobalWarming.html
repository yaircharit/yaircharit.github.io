How to use

  • 1 Step: advance the simulation by one tick.
  • Run: start running continuously (updates every ~250ms).
  • Stop: stop the continuous run.
  • Pollution Modifier: change how much pollution each tile type generates/absorbs.
  • env.*: tweak pollution and temperature thresholds (freeze/scorch/deforest/etc.) that control environment transitions.
  • Chart: shows average temperature, pollution, and cities over time.
  • Zoom: zoom in and out of the map.
Tip: Press Esc to close this window.
